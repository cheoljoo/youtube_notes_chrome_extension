Privacy Policy

This is the Privacy Policy for the YouTube Notes extension (hereinafter "Extension"). We take the protection of your personal information very seriously.

1. Information We Collect

This extension only collects the following information:

  • YouTube Page Metadata: Video title, publish date, and URL
  • User Input: Tags and opinion notes (manually entered by user)
  • Timestamps: Time when notes are saved

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Browsing history
  • Personally identifiable information
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Location information

2. How We Store Data

All data is stored only on your local device:

  • Chrome Local Storage: Note data (chrome.storage.local)
  • Chrome Sync Storage: Tag list (chrome.storage.sync - synced with your Chrome account)

We do not transmit data to external servers.

3. Third-Party Sharing

This extension:

  • Does not share user data with third parties
  • Does not use advertising networks or analytics services
  • Does not transmit data to remote servers

4. CSV Export

Users can export all of their data at any time using the "Download CSV" feature. This is completely under your control.

5. Extension Removal

When you remove this extension, Chrome automatically deletes all associated data.

6. Permission Explanations

  • storage: To store your notes locally
  • activeTab: To access metadata from the current YouTube page
  • scripting: To extract video title and publish date from the page

7. Policy Changes

If this policy changes, we will notify you through a new version of the extension.

8. Contact

For any questions regarding privacy, please contact us through GitHub issues.

Summary: YouTube Notes stores your data locally only and does not transmit it externally or share it with third parties.
